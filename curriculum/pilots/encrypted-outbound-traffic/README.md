# Encrypted Outbound Traffic: Decide Before You Disrupt

## Your situation

You are the network analyst supporting a cyber defense team. Monitoring has identified sustained encrypted outbound traffic from a workstation shortly after a scheduled software update.

The destination is new, the payload is encrypted, and an operational decision is waiting:

> Is the traffic reasonably explained by authorized software activity, or does the available evidence justify containment or escalation?

Your job is not to label the traffic from one indicator. Your job is to make the strongest decision the evidence supports, explain what remains uncertain, and give the next team something useful to act on.

## What you will do

Allow about 60–90 minutes. No account, paid resource, specialized platform, or installed analysis tool is required.

1. Read the [initial evidence packet](evidence-packet.md).
2. Produce a concise initial assessment without opening the feedback file.
3. Open the [follow-up evidence and transfer check](feedback-and-transfer.md).
4. Revise your assessment and complete the short transfer case.
5. Use the [review guide](review-guide.md) for feedback or self-review.

If a concept is unfamiliar, consult the optional [just-in-time scaffolds](scaffolds.md). Use only what helps you reason through the situation.

## Initial work product

Use any clear format. Aim for one or two pages, not a long report. Make these elements inspectable:

- **Situation:** What decision must be made, for whom, and why now?
- **Observations:** Which facts matter, where did they come from, and what are their limitations?
- **Interpretations:** What might those observations mean? Keep these distinct from the observations themselves.
- **Plausible explanations:** Compare at least two explanations, including evidence for and against each.
- **Next evidence:** Identify the two or three checks most likely to change the decision. Explain why they come first.
- **Assessment:** State what is most likely, your confidence, and the important uncertainty.
- **Action and handoff:** Recommend a proportionate next action and state what another role needs from you.

Do not search for the fictional company, product, domain, or address. They intentionally cannot be validated on the public internet. You may look up unfamiliar technical concepts, but outside information does not replace reasoning from the supplied evidence.

## Stop point

Complete and save your initial work product before opening `feedback-and-transfer.md`. The later evidence is deliberately unavailable during the initial decision.
